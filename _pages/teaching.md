## Arizona State University

### GIS 521: GIS Programming *(Graduate)*
*School of Geographical Sciences and Urban Planning*

An applied graduate course covering Python-based GIS programming, including spatial data manipulation, remote sensing workflows, and building reproducible geospatial pipelines. Topics include GeoPandas, Rasterio, spatial analysis and machine learning. Students complete a final project applying these skills to a problem of their choosing.

**Topics covered:**
- Python for spatial data (GeoPandas, Shapely, Fiona)
- Raster analysis with Rasterio and GEE
- High-Perofmance Computing for GIS
- Introduction to GeoAI
