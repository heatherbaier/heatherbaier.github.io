## Arizona State University

### GIS 521: GIS Programming *(Graduate)*
*School of Geographical Sciences and Urban Planning*

An applied graduate course covering Python-based GIS programming, including spatial data manipulation, remote sensing workflows, and building reproducible geospatial pipelines. Topics include GeoPandas, Rasterio, Google Earth Engine, and spatial analysis with scikit-learn. Students complete a final project applying these skills to a problem of their choosing.

**Topics covered:**
- Python for spatial data (GeoPandas, Shapely, Fiona)
- Raster analysis with Rasterio and GEE
- Urban heat island analysis (replicating Sidiqui et al., 2022, applied to Phoenix)
- Geographically Weighted Regression (GWR)
- Introduction to GeoAI
